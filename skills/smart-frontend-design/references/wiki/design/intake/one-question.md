## Ask One Design-Essence Question

Before building a component, page, or app, ask exactly one pushback or
context-gathering question about the design essence. The question should expose
the desired tone, audience, or differentiator, not collect routine specs.

**Incorrect:**

```markdown
What colors, fonts, layout, animations, spacing, breakpoints, and component
states do you want?
```

This turns design into a form and leaves the agent free to average the answers
into generic output.

**Correct:**

```markdown
What should this feel like in one sentence: brutal, luxe, playful, editorial,
raw, calm, weird, or something else?
```

If the user already gave a strong style cue, brand reference, screenshot,
audience, or emotional target, treat that as the answer and do not ask again.
Proceed to discovery and synthesis.
