## Commit To A Bold Aesthetic Direction

Pick a clear aesthetic thesis before implementation. The direction can be
brutally minimal, maximalist chaos, retro-futuristic, organic, luxury, playful,
editorial, brutalist, art deco, soft pastel, industrial, or something more
specific to the product. The point is intentionality.

**Incorrect:**

```markdown
Design direction: clean, modern, intuitive, and professional.
```

Those words do not constrain typography, color, motion, composition, or the
memorable detail.

**Correct:**

```markdown
Design direction: "quiet aerospace archive" - matte graphite surfaces, narrow
technical type, blueprint hairlines, slow parallax star charts, and one amber
system-status accent.
```

Bold maximalism and refined minimalism both work. Match the execution to the
thesis: maximalism earns elaborate motion and layering; minimalism earns
obsessive spacing, type hierarchy, and restraint.
