## False Ranges

AI uses "from X to Y" where X and Y aren't on a meaningful scale. Real ranges have endpoints on a spectrum. Fake ones are just lists dressed up.

**Incorrect:**

```markdown
Our journey has taken us from the singularity of the Big Bang to the grand cosmic web, from the birth of stars to the enigmatic dance of dark matter.
```

**Correct:**

```markdown
The book covers the Big Bang, star formation, and current theories about dark matter.
```

Rule: if X and Y aren't literal endpoints of a measurable scale, don't write "from X to Y." Just list the items.
