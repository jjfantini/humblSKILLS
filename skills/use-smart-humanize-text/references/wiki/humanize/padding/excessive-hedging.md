## Excessive Hedging

AI over-qualifies statements to avoid committing. Stacking modals ("could potentially possibly") signals no conviction.

**Incorrect:**

```markdown
It could potentially possibly be argued that the policy might have some effect.
```

**Correct:**

```markdown
The policy may affect outcomes.
```

Rule: one hedge per claim, maximum. "May" OR "might" OR "could," not all three. If you're unsure, state the uncertainty once and move on.
