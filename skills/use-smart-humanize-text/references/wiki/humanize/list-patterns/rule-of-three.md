## Rule of Three

AI defaults to lists of exactly three items, especially adjectives. Humans use one or two. Prefer one concrete claim.

**Incorrect:**

```markdown
a dynamic, innovative, and forward-thinking company
```

**Correct:**

```markdown
a company that ships fast
```

If you need a list, vary the length (1, 2, 4, or 5 items). If you find yourself stacking three adjectives, pick the one that's actually true and cut the others.
