## Negative Parallelisms

"Not just X, but also Y" and "It's not X - it's Y." Cut the negation and state what the thing actually is.

**Incorrect:**

```markdown
It's not just a platform - it's a movement.
```

**Correct:**

Say what makes it different in concrete terms. Or cut it.

```markdown
Users organize protests, recruit volunteers, and coordinate rides through the platform.
```

Rule: if you wrote "not just," you're trying to manufacture contrast that doesn't exist. State the actual distinguishing facts.
