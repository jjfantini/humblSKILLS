## Hard-Ban Words

Statistically overrepresented in AI output. Do not use unless quoting someone or the word is genuinely the only correct choice.

**Never use:**

delve, tapestry, vibrant, foster/fostering, underscore, showcase/showcasing, pivotal, meticulous/meticulously, intricate/intricacies, enduring, bolstered, garner, interplay, testament, landscape (abstract), nestled, in the heart of, groundbreaking, renowned, indelible mark, rich (as filler adjective), profound, boasts (meaning "has"), exemplifies, seamless, catalyze/catalyst

See `plain-replacements.md` for canonical 1:1 substitutes.
