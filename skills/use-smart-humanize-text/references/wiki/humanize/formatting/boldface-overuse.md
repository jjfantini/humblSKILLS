## Boldface Overuse

AI mechanically bolds every keyword. Humans bold sparingly, for genuine emphasis.

**Incorrect:**

```markdown
A **leveraged buyout (LBO)** uses **debt financing** by **private equity firms** to control **target companies**.
```

**Correct:**

```markdown
A leveraged buyout (LBO) uses debt financing to acquire a company. The buyer puts up a small amount of equity; the rest is borrowed.
```

Rule: bold at most one or two terms per paragraph, and only when the reader would miss the point without the visual emphasis. If every noun is bold, nothing is emphasized.
