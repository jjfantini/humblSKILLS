## Sentence Rhythm

AI tends toward uniform cadence: similar length, similar structure, uniform contractions. Humans are inconsistent. Lean into it.

### Moves to use

- **Vary rhythm.** Short. Then a long one that takes its time. Then medium again.
- **Start sentences with "And," "But," "So."** AI rarely does this. Humans do it constantly.
- **Use contractions inconsistently.** "I am" in one sentence, "I've" in the next. AI is uniform.
- **Repeat words.** Don't synonym-cycle. If the thing is "the platform," call it "the platform" every time. See `word-variation/elegant-variation.md`.

Rule: if three consecutive sentences have similar length and structure, break the pattern - join two with a comma, or chop one into a fragment.
