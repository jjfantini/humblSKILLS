## Signs of Soulless Writing

Avoiding AI patterns is half the job. Sterile, voiceless writing is just as obvious. Even if the text is "clean," it can still read as algorithmic.

Check for:

- Every sentence is the same length and structure
- No opinions, just neutral reporting
- No acknowledgment of uncertainty or mixed feelings
- No first-person when appropriate
- No humor, no edge, no personality
- Reads like a press release

If three or more of these are true, the text has no pulse. See `adding-personality.md` and `sentence-rhythm.md` for fixes.
