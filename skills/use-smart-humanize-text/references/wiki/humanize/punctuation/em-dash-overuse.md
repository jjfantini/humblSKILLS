## Em Dash Overuse

AI uses em dashes in a formulaic "setup - punchline" pattern where commas, parentheses, or periods work fine.

**Incorrect:**

```markdown
The result was clear - a complete transformation of the business.
```

**Correct:**

```markdown
The result was clear. The business completely changed.
```

Rule: default to a period. Use a dash only when the break is sharper than a comma and the fragment after it is essential. If you're hitting more than one em dash per paragraph, you're AI-pattern-matching.
