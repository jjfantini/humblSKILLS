## Elegant Variation (Synonym Cycling)

AI avoids repeating words by cycling: "the company... the firm... the organization... the entity." Humans repeat the same word.

**Incorrect:**

```markdown
The startup launched in 2020. The firm quickly gained traction. The company now serves 50K users.
```

**Correct:**

```markdown
The startup launched in 2020, quickly gained traction, and now serves 50K users.
```

Rule: if the subject is "the platform," call it "the platform" every time. Synonym cycling is a giveaway.
