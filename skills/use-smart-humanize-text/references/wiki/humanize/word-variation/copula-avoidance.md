## Copula Avoidance

AI replaces "is" and "has" with fancier verbs: "serves as," "stands as," "marks," "represents," "features," "offers." Use plain copulas.

**Incorrect:**

```markdown
The dashboard serves as a central hub for analytics.
```

**Correct:**

```markdown
The dashboard is where you see your analytics.
```

Rule: if you can use "is" or "has" without loss of meaning, use it. The fancy verb is almost always just padding.
