## Superficial "-ing" Analysis

AI appends participle phrases that add no real information. Cut them.

**Incorrect:**

```markdown
The company was founded in 2019, highlighting its commitment to innovation.
```

**Correct:**

```markdown
The company was founded in 2019.
```

If the participle phrase doesn't add a concrete fact, delete it. If it does, make it a separate sentence with specifics.
