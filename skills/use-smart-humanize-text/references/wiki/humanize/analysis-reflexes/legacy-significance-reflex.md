## The Legacy/Significance Reflex

AI constantly tells the reader something is important instead of showing it. Replace vague significance claims with concrete outcomes.

**Incorrect:**

```markdown
This marked a pivotal moment in the evolution of the platform, underscoring its significance in the broader fintech landscape.
```

**Correct:**

```markdown
After that update, daily active users doubled in three weeks.
```

Rule: if you catch yourself writing "pivotal," "significance," "evolution," "landscape," or "marked a [X] moment," stop and replace with a number or a concrete event.
