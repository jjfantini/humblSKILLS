## The "Despite Challenges" Formula

AI ends sections with "Despite [positive stuff], [subject] faces challenges... Despite these challenges, [positive outlook]." Name specific problems instead.

**Incorrect:**

```markdown
Despite its rapid growth, the team faces challenges including scaling infrastructure and regulatory compliance. Despite these challenges, the company continues to innovate.
```

**Correct:**

```markdown
Scaling infrastructure is the current bottleneck. Regulatory compliance in three jurisdictions is eating engineering time.
```

If a real problem exists, describe it specifically. If there's no real problem, don't manufacture a fake tension to resolve.
