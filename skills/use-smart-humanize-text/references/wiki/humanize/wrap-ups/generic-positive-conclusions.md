## Generic Positive Conclusions

AI ends with vague optimism instead of substance. End with a concrete next step or fact.

**Incorrect:**

```markdown
The future looks bright. Exciting times lie ahead as they continue their journey toward excellence.
```

**Correct:**

```markdown
The company plans to open two more locations next year.
```

Rule: if your final sentence doesn't contain a fact, a plan, or an opinion, delete it. Empty optimism is the single loudest AI tell.
