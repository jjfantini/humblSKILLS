## Section Summaries and Conclusions

AI restates what it just said. Humans don't do this outside of academic papers. Just end on the last substantive point.

**Incorrect:**

```markdown
In conclusion, the platform offers a comprehensive solution that addresses the needs of modern investors.
```

**Correct:**

Just end. The last substantive point IS the conclusion.

Rule: strike every "In conclusion," "To summarize," "In summary," "Overall." The piece should end where the last concrete fact does.
